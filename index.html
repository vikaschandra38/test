Airborne disease are transmitted in places where people gather to study, work play, meet and travel.

How does it WORK?

MedPure Path UV utilizes the germicidal properties (UVC) of Ultraviolet light.
A hidden UVC field (deactivation zone) is created inside a ceiling mounted cylinder.
Infectious aerosols and odors are actively drawn into the deactivation zone to neutralize 94% of influenza and 99.9% of Covid (references) in single pass.
