How does it WORK?

MedPure Path UV works on the concept of Ultraviolet Germicidal irradiation (UCGI). It is a ceiling mounted unit with a fan on top a cylinder that creates a hidden de-contamination zone, drawing infectious aerosols into the de-contamination zone and releasing clean air - eliminating 94% influenza and 99.9% of covid in one pass.
